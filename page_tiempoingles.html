What are the verb tenses in English

In English there are three basic verb tenses: past, present and future. These in turn have variables. With the variables they add approximately 24 verb tenses, between active and passive.

Since passive verb tenses are of very little use, even some without use, we will focus more on the active ones. As an example we will use the verb "watch".
